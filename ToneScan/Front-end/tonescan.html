ToneScan

ToneScan is a website for finding out the skin tone. This service may help you assess skin spots and moles for the most common types of skin tones.

Sign up Read more

How to use

Take a photo of the face with your smartphone and upload it or you can take a picture using our take capture option. This website will scan your image within 30 seconds. And it will show the results.

How we do it?

Machine Learning is used to detect the colour of the skin.It scans the image and according to the rgb value it gives the result of the skin colour.
